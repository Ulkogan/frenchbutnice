about

Olivier is a creative working at the edge of technology. He leads AI design at Google Gemini, where he has spent the past four years building generative UI frameworks. Previously at Frame.io (acquired by Adobe) and Airbnb. Fifteen years of shipping the infrastructure that makes creative teams faster — and is now reshaping what creative even means.

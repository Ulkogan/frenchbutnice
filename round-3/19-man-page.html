DESCRIPTION

Olivier Gillaizeau is a creative working at the edge of technology, currently leading AI design at Google Gemini. Fifteen years of shipping the infrastructure that makes creative teams faster — and is now reshaping what creative even means.

He started in Flash, scaled creative organizations across continents, directed global campaigns at Airbnb, launched Camera-to-Cloud at Frame.io, and now builds generative interfaces for the model that lives inside Gemini. Designer. Systems lead. AI practitioner. French, but nice.

This is a builder's voice, not a thinkpiece voice. Most of the industry is still debating whether AI is coming. Olivier has been shipping it.

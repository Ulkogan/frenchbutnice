What I tend to think about

Two beds, mostly. design systems at scale— adoption over perfection, coherence over uniformity, systems as services rather than mandates; fifteen years of watching what breaks past the hundredth team. And designing with ai, not around it— the practitioner view from inside Gemini, where generative UI stopped being a demo and became something a design system has to host as a first-class citizen.

Come talk about it

I give talks and workshops on exactly these two subjects — Design Systems at Scale, about what breaks at 600+ teams and how to build for adoption instead of enforcement; and Designing with Generative AI, from Gemini's Canvases to the generative UI frameworks that change what a design system is for. If either is useful to your team or event, email me — og@frenchbutnice.design.
